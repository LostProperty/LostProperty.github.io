We bring our client's digital products and services to life.

Who we are.

We are a team of veteran developers and technologists who build usable software for the web and iOS.

We love to build unique products that people want to use.

What do we offer?

We normally help our clients in one of the following ways:

Design and develop new products

This ranges from prototyping an idea to test the market (warblr) to complete build of a proven idea (catinaflat)

Give a spark to an existing product

Sometimes an existing product or site has fantastic potential but needs a shot in the arm. Or perhaps your site is performing well but the time is right to make some changes. We worked with our friends at Razorfish to get allthingshair.com ready for delivery and nurture it through its first year all the while making incremental improvements that ranged from improved search and internationalisation across multiple global markets to developing a complete UX revamp of the admin system.

Post launch

Although sometimes overlooked, what happens after a site goes live can be just as critical as the initial design and build. The web is a living thing and websites often need to adapt, often in subtle ways, to keep up. Whether it's tweaking server infrastructure to better meet the service's demands or just staying on top of security updates we ensure that everything we build keeps running smoothly.

Our work

We've worked with companies ranging in size from two-person startups to multinational, FTSE 100 giants.

  • All Things Hair

    Allthingshair.com is a multi-market youtube channel that features styling tips from a variety of celebs and well-known fashion bloggers.

    We teamed up with razorfish.com to help them get allthingshair.com ready for launch and since then have helped guide it to meet its promise. We've added support for more international markets (with new markets begin added all the time) which is great for the end user and have both created new tooling as well as improving the existing ones for the site's admins. Sometimes it seems as if the importance of non public facing portions of the site can be underestimated but Razorfish fully understand the importance of a great experice from front-to-back. We're very proud of everything about this site.

  • Cat in a Flat

    Our friends at catinaflat.com have a long running cat sitting business that they have carefully built up over the last few years. They approached us to help them create a new version of their existing site that would help put cat sitters in touch with cats that need sitting.

    What we love about catinaflat.com is the focus on the user. They want to ensure that the person you entrust your cat to is a reliable person and so they go above and beyond in thoroughly interviewing and performing a background check on any potential cat sitters.

  • Warblr

    We worked with the incredibly clever team at Warblr to put togther the first version of their ios app. Warblr is an audio recognition app for birdsong; you simply record the calls of one our feathery friends on your mobile device and Warble can identify the species of bird as well as provide information about that species.

  • Seabeard

    Making video games has to be the glamour job of the software industry and while we can't lay claim to having built any of the gameplay mechanics we are very proud to have been involved in the making of Seabeard.

    We've known the guys from Handcircus.com for a while now and so when they asked if we would be available to build the online backend for Seabeard we jumped at the chance. This was quite a task as it involves massively multiplayer gameplay and required highly performant services that worked well across mobile devices with all of the vagaries of mobile networks. Check out Seabeard now on the Apple appstore.

  • AMV BBDO Graduate

    Over at AMV BBDO they are very keen to develop the digital industry's next generation of talent and part of that is their graduate scheme. We built the graduate.amvbbdo.com site to facilitate the application process for graduates as well as the reporting tools to smooth the process for those on the other side of the curtain who review the applications.
